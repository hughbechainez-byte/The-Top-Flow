The Top Flow offline rhyme candidate ranking uses derived English word-frequency scores
from wordfreq (https://github.com/rspeer/wordfreq), version 3.1.1, at build time only.

wordfreq code is Apache-2.0. Its included frequency data is redistributable under
CC BY-SA 4.0 and includes data derived from Google Books Ngrams, Leeds Internet Corpus,
and SUBTLEX frequency lists. See https://pypi.org/project/wordfreq/ for attribution details.

The app continues to include CMUdict pronunciation data. CMUdict is maintained by the
Carnegie Mellon University Speech Group and is suitable for unrestricted research and
commercial use; see https://github.com/cmusphinx/cmudict.
